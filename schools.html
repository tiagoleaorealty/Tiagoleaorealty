Schools in Guanacaste.

Where families relocating to Guanacaste actually send their kids — IB, Montessori, Waldorf and bilingual programs, with what each one publishes about itself.

Costa Rica has no equivalent of the US school ratings families are used to, so there are no scores on this page. What is here is what each school states publicly: grade levels, curriculum, languages, and accreditation. Where a school does not publish something, it is left blank rather than guessed at.

Every listing on this site shows the schools nearest to it. If you are weighing a particular town, the school is usually the constraint that decides it — not the house.

From choosing a school to choosing a home

For relocating families, the right property often depends on much more than the home itself. School schedules, realistic daily drive times, children's ages, work locations, and access to services all affect where it makes sense to live. I help families compare the practical relationship between schools and communities, organize property tours, and build a housing shortlist around everyday life — not only the look of a house.

When available, I can point you to the right admissions contact. Tuition, enrollment, curriculum, transportation, and availability should always be confirmed directly with each school.
