## Immigration (residency article) — verify with an immigration attorney
- Inversionista: property must be held **personally** (not via corporation) to qualify; $150,000 threshold current under Law 9996
- Rentista: $2,500/mo unearned income OR $60,000 deposit structure; ongoing $2,500/mo exchange obligation
- Pensionado: $1,000/mo lifetime pension; 401(k)/IRA withdrawals do NOT qualify
- Work restrictions during temporary residency; remote-work gray area framing
- Permanent residency after 3 years; citizenship after 7 (+Spanish/civics test); dual citizenship allowed
- Tourist entry 180 days; legal stay after filing application
- CAJA: 9–11% of declared income; example amounts ($100–150 pensionado, $200–350 rentista); free spouse/child coverage
